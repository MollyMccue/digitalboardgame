About:

The inspiration for the board game comes from a combination of candy
land and scp. The SCP Foundation is a fictional secret organization documented
by the collaborative-writing wiki project of the same name. SCP simply means
secure, contain and protect. This game was created to add a twist on your
average candy land.

How to Play

This game is not played like the usual candy land. Since there is a twist on the
game there is also a twist on the ways to play and rules. When entering the game
choose character. Then draw your card. The round your playing and show turn it
is will show. The point of this game is to escape the scp. To move you will be
showed a card with a color on it. If it has one move to the color. If it has two of
the same colors on it move two of those colors. If you get a card with a flashlight,
mask move to the square no matter where you are. If you get on any of the traps
your turn gets skipped.
